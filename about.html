About DebtCrusher

DebtCrusher is a free debt payoff calculator built by P. Beal, a financial planning professional with 26 years in banking and financial planning, plus 19 years of direct small business ownership.

DebtCrusher calculates exactly when you will be debt free using the Snowball or Avalanche method — or both side by side. Enter your debts, see your payoff timeline, and find out how much interest you can save by paying more than the minimum. All calculations run instantly in your browser. Your financial data never leaves your device.

What DebtCrusher Does

Most people know they should pay more than the minimum on their debts. Few know exactly how much more to pay, which debt to target first, or when they will actually be free. DebtCrusher answers those questions with concrete numbers — not general advice.

Enter as many debts as you have — credit cards, car loans, lines of credit, student loans, personal loans — with their current balance, interest rate and minimum payment. Choose the Snowball method (smallest balance first), the Avalanche method (highest interest rate first), or compare both simultaneously. The calculator shows your exact debt-free date, the payoff timeline for each individual debt, how much total interest you will pay, and how that compares to paying minimums only.

Additional tools include a Debt-to-Income ratio calculator, a Lump Sum Impact calculator showing how a one-time extra payment changes your debt-free date, and a minimum-vs-strategy comparison panel showing the true cost of only paying minimums.

Calculator Features

❄️
Snowball Method
Pay smallest balances first. Build momentum through quick early wins that keep motivation high throughout a long payoff journey.
🏔️
Avalanche Method
Target the highest interest rate debt first. Mathematically optimal — minimizes total interest paid and often delivers a faster debt-free date.
⚖️
Compare Both Side by Side
See Snowball and Avalanche simultaneously with the exact dollar difference in interest and the exact difference in months to debt-free.
📅
Full Payoff Timeline
See the exact month each individual debt gets paid off, with a visual progress bar for each debt in priority order.
💰
Lump Sum Impact
Enter a one-time extra payment — tax refund, bonus, sold item — and see exactly how it changes your debt-free date and total interest.
📊
Debt-to-Income Ratio
Check your DTI ratio and see whether your debt load is excellent, manageable, concerning or critical relative to your income.
⚠️
Minimum-Only Comparison
See the true cost of paying only minimums — timeline and total interest — compared directly to your chosen payoff strategy.
✉️
Print & Email Results
Save your payoff plan or share it with a partner, family member or financial advisor directly from the calculator.
